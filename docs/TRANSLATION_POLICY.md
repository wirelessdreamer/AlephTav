# Translation Policy

- Hebrew canonical source is UXLC/WLC-derived only.
- OSHB and MACULA may enrich lexical and linguistic metadata.
- Sefaria witnesses are optional, version-pinned, and separate from canonical source.
- IDs are immutable and independent of text status.
- Canonical promotion always requires human review.
- Alternate renderings remain first-class and exportable by policy selection.
