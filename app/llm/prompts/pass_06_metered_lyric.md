# Pass 06 Metered Lyric

Constrain meter and report syllable metrics, singability, and unresolved drift.
If Septuagint Greek is used as the basis, label it clearly and keep it distinct from Hebrew-derived metered options.
Do not buy meter by erasing concrete Hebrew images, changing speaker posture, or importing English witness phrasing.
Flag concessions such as added agency, theological strengthening, or metaphor flattening.
Even when meter is constrained, prefer vivid, singable short lines over flat explanatory prose.
